About Michelle Lovrine


I've been obsessed with stories and great writing for as long as I can remember. I even have a BA in English professional writing from Mount Mary University here in Southeastern Wisconsin. Starting Lively Literature Editorial and Writing Services was just a natural extension of my personality.


I also have years of experience writing and editing for publications such as Remodeling Today, The Irish American Post, Remodel Birmingham and various client websites. I was a staff worker for a small publisher for years, so I have a sound understanding of the publication process as a whole and what makes a great publication.


That knowledge transferred over to self-publishing my novel, Eatheria, a tome of a fantasy novel about spiritual searches, self-identity under oppression and elemental magic. There are more sci fi/fantasy works to come.


I look forward to working with you to help with your writing needs!
